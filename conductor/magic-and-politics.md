# Eldoria: Magic Typology & Political Systems

Based on the project's strategic planning, Eldoria features a comprehensive, multi-layered approach to both its magical architecture and its political simulation.

## 1. Thematic Magic Typology (The Four Currents)
Magic in Eldoria is categorized into four "Currents." These currents combine the modern, scientific understanding of energy (The "Isekai" perspective) with the traditional elemental manifestations found in classic anime and fantasy.

1. **Thermal Entropy (The Scorch):** The manipulation of energy states and particle excitation.
    *   **Elemental Manifestations:** Fire (Excitation), Ice (Depletion), and Lightning (Ionization).
    *   **Industrial Tooling:** Brass flamethrowers, coolant coils, static-induction batteries.
2. **Vector Resonance (The Force):** The manipulation of physical direction, mass, and gravimetric weight.
    *   **Elemental Manifestations:** Earth (Mass manipulation), Wind (Pressure vectors), and Gravity (Spatial anchoring).
    *   **Industrial Tooling:** Repulsor gauntlets, magnetic accelerators, gravity-anchors.
3. **Biomorphic Flux (The Bloom):** The manipulation of cellular regeneration and organic breakdown.
    *   **Elemental Manifestations:** Nature (Growth), Poison/Miasma (Decay), and Holy (Purification/Regeneration).
    *   **Industrial Tooling:** Chemical injectors, life-force siphons, aerosolized toxin canisters.
4. **Cognitive Distortion (The Echo):** The manipulation of sensory input and neural pathways.
    *   **Elemental Manifestations:** Light (Visual distortion), Shadow (Sensory deprivation), and Psychic (Neural interface).
    *   **Industrial Tooling:** Phonic disruptors, memory-siphons, hallucinogenic smog emitters.

## 2. The Integrated Political System (The Tri-Axis Power)
The political simulation is a **non-exclusive, open-ended web**. Players are not locked into a single "path." They can intermingle across all three layers simultaneously, with actions in one tier causing cascading consequences in others.

### Tier 1: Faction Warfare & Bounties (Direct Power)
- **Mechanics:** Direct conflict with the Syndicate or Adepts. Capturing outposts, clearing bandit camps.
- **Intermingling:** Use your "Hero" status from warfare to gain public trust for elections (Tier 2).

### Tier 2: Civic Governance (Systemic Power)
- **Mechanics:** Becoming an official leader (Mayor, Governor). Setting laws, taxes, and controlling the town guard.
- **Intermingling:** Pass laws that secretly benefit your underworld smuggling routes (Tier 3).

### Tier 3: Underworld Brokerage (Shadow Power)
- **Mechanics:** Blackmail, election rigging, and political funding.
- **Intermingling:** Fund a rival to unseat a Mayor (Tier 2) who is being too "pure" for your business.

### The Consequences of "Total Play"
Players face the "Systemic Collapse" risk. The more tiers you control, the harder it is to keep your secrets. If caught, your Alignment and Purity will crash, and the world's reaction will be swift and violent.
