# Storylet: The Borderlands Wake

**Location:** The Borderlands - Wreckage of the [Player Vehicle]
**Characters:** The Player, Kaelen (Scavenger)

---

The world begins in a haze of acrid smoke and the smell of ozone. You are lying on your back, the cold, red dust of the Borderlands seeping into your clothes. Above you, the sky is a bruised purple, flickering with distant, silent lightning—the "Static" of the dead zones.

A few yards away, your vehicle—an impossible machine of smooth glass and painted steel—is a twisted wreck. Its engine is dead, yet it seems to vibrate with a frequency that doesn't belong in this world.

"By the Unseen... it's solid. It's actually solid."

A voice, raspy and low, cuts through the ringing in your ears. A figure is crouched by the wreckage. This is Kaelen, a "Blank Canvas" scavenger, dressed in layers of quilted iron and leather. On their hip hangs a clunky, brass-bound "Resonance Torch" that flickers with a weak, dying light.

Kaelen isn't looking at you. Their eyes are wide, fixed on your vehicle's shattered dashboard. Specifically, they are staring at your **smartphone** (or a similar modern device) that has fallen into the dust. To you, it's a piece of junk. To them, it is a relic of "Perfect Tech"—it has no spirit inside, yet it held a charge. It is a "Null" object.

"Stranger," Kaelen finally notices you, their hand drifting toward a rusted iron knife. "I don't know what world you fell from, but that 'Glass Slate' in the dirt... it's worth more than a Resonance Well in the Nexus. If the Syndicate scouts saw your streak in the sky, they'll be here in ten ticks."

Kaelen stands up, looking between you and the wreckage. "You look like you've been chewed up by the Breach. I can help you move, but my Crawler is low on shards. If you want a ride out of this Static, you're going to have to give me something to trade."

---

### **The First Choice: The Awakening of the Echo-Anchor**

1. **[RESONANCE] "The spirit in your torch... it's fading. Let me help."**
   * *Action:* You reach out. Your "Echo-Anchor" instinctively pulses. You don't "use" magic; you simply **stabilize** the flickering spirit in Kaelen's torch, restoring it to a steady, warm glow.
   * *Reaction:* Kaelen stumbles back, eyes wide. "What... what did you just do? You didn't even use a Focus..."
   * *Effect:* +10 Sync, +5 Prowess. Kaelen is terrified but grateful.

2. **[DISTORTION] "Help me up, or you'll never touch that glass slate again."**
   * *Action:* You lean on your modern knowledge of physics. You grab a piece of the wreckage—a live wire from the car battery—and spark it against your metal fender. The violent, non-magical electricity scares the scavenger.
   * *Reaction:* Kaelen snarls, but lowers the knife. "A feisty one. Fine. But the Syndicate won't be as easy to scare as a border-rat like me."
   * *Effect:* +10 Logic, -5 Purity. Kaelen respects your ruthlessness.

3. **[THE VOID] (Silently grab the smartphone and stand up).**
   * *Action:* You ignore the talk. You grab your device—the only thing that anchors you to your home—and stare down the scavenger.
   * *Reaction:* Kaelen spits in the dust. "Keep your secrets, then. But the Static is getting thicker. If we don't move, the Blighted will smell your blood."
   * *Effect:* +5 Finesse. No immediate alignment shift.
