# Storylet: The Borderlands Wake

**Location:** The Borderlands - The Static Crater
**Characters:** The Player, Kaelen (Scavenger)

---

The world begins in a haze of acrid smoke and the smell of ozone. You are lying at the bottom of a shallow, glass-edged crater. The red dust of the Borderlands is swirling around you, but within the circle of the crater, everything is eerily still. The "Static" lightning of the dead zones arcs across the sky, but it refuses to touch the ground where you lie.

You have no vehicle, no tools, nothing but the clothes on your back—which feel strangely heavy and "too real" for this flickering world.

"By the Unseen... it's a Null-Fall. A clean one."

A voice, raspy and low, cuts through the ringing in your ears. A figure is peering over the edge of the crater. This is Kaelen, a "Blank Canvas" scavenger, dressed in layers of quilted iron and leather. On their hip hangs a clunky, brass-bound "Resonance Torch" that has gone completely dark.

Kaelen isn't looking for scrap. They are staring at **you**. To them, you are the relic. You have no spirit inside you, no resonance, yet you are breathing. You are a "Null" anomaly.

"Stranger," Kaelen calls down, their hand drifting toward a rusted iron knife. "I don't know what void you leaked out of, but the fact that you're standing in a dead-zone without screaming... that's worth more than a Resonance Well in the Nexus. If the Syndicate scouts saw your streak in the sky, they'll be here in ten ticks."

Kaelen slides down into the crater, looking you up and down. "My torch died the second you hit the ground. You've sucked the life right out of the air. If you want a ride out of here before the Blighted show up, you're going to have to prove you aren't just a walking curse."

---

### **The First Choice: The Awakening of the Echo-Anchor**

1. **[RESONANCE] "Your torch isn't dead. It's just... confused. Let me help."**
   * *Action:* You reach out. Your "Echo-Anchor" instinctively pulses. You don't "use" magic; you simply **stabilize** the static air in the crater, allowing Kaelen's torch to flicker back to a steady, warm glow.
   * *Reaction:* Kaelen stumbles back, eyes wide. "What... what did you just do? You didn't even use a Focus..."
   * *Effect:* +10 Sync, +5 Prowess. Kaelen is terrified but grateful.

2. **[DISTORTION] "I'm not a curse. I'm the only thing in this desert that makes sense. Help me up."**
   * *Action:* You use your "Modern Perspective." You point out the physical flaw in Kaelen's gear—a loose wire or a jammed gear that has nothing to do with magic. Your confidence in "Physics" over "Spirits" intimidates them.
   * *Reaction:* Kaelen snarls, but lowers the knife. "A smart one. Fine. But the Syndicate won't be as easy to scare as a border-rat like me."
   * *Effect:* +10 Logic, -5 Purity. Kaelen respects your authority.

3. **[THE VOID] (Silently stand up and climb out of the crater).**
   * *Action:* You ignore the talk. You trust your own strength. You scramble up the glass-slick walls of the crater, ignoring the scavenger's questions.
   * *Reaction:* Kaelen spits in the dust. "Keep your secrets, then. But the Static is getting thicker. If we don't move, the Blighted will smell your blood."
   * *Effect:* +5 Finesse. No immediate alignment shift.
