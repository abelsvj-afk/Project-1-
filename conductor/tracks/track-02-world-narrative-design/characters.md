# Core Character Dossiers

This document details the primary non-player characters (NPCs), their backgrounds, and their seeded personality traits for the procedural text engine.

## Overseer Vane (The Iron Syndicate)

**Role:** Commander of "The Iron Watch," the Syndicate's outpost in the Borderlands.
**Alignment Bias:** Highly Logic-driven, Neutral Alignment, Corrupt-leaning (pragmatic, uses people as resources).

**Background:** 
Vane is a career bureaucrat who views his assignment to the Borderlands as an insult. He is not a warrior by trade, but an administrator who relies entirely on "Arcane-Industrial" tech. He possesses no natural magic, which makes him fiercely protective of the Syndicate's technological superiority. To Vane, spirits are nothing more than batteries; raw material to be refined and consumed for order.

**Motivations:**
1. Secure the "Null" anomaly (the Player's crash site) to earn a promotion back to the Nexus.
2. Suppress the Adepts, whom he views as dangerous, unpredictable anarchists.

**Seeded Personality Matrix (For Text Engine):**
- *Tone:* Clinical, precise, dismissive, tired.
- *Vocabulary Focus:* Metrics, quotas, efficiency, protocol, static, yields.
- *Visual Tells:* Adjusting his brass monocle, checking his pocket-watch, wiping red dust from his pristine coat.

**Example Procedural Dialogue Base:**
> "Your presence here is a statistical anomaly. Anomalies reduce efficiency. Explain your function before I have you processed for scrap."

## Elder Syla (The Silent Anchor)

**Role:** Spiritual leader of the Adept pilgrimage camp in the Borderlands.
**Alignment Bias:** Highly Sync-driven, Good-leaning, Pure-leaning (traditionalist, deeply connected to spirits).

**Background:**
Syla is one of the "Fading Nobility," families whose bloodlines were naturally attuned to Aetheria's spirits long before the tech revolution. She views the Iron Syndicate's methods as a horrific desecration, akin to burning souls for fuel. She is stationed in the Borderlands to stabilize "Static Spots" (areas drained of magic by over-industrialization). 

**Motivations:**
1. Heal the "Static Crater" caused by the Player's arrival.
2. Determine if the Player is a prophesied "Void-Walker" or simply a dangerous weapon built by the Syndicate.

**Seeded Personality Matrix (For Text Engine):**
- *Tone:* Patient, sorrowful, intense, slightly condescending to the non-magical.
- *Vocabulary Focus:* Resonance, echoes, the Fall, breath, harmony, distortion.
- *Visual Tells:* Closing her eyes to "listen" to the air, her hands glowing with soft, stabilizing light, a constant faint hum of wind around her.

**Example Procedural Dialogue Base:**
> "The air around you does not sing, traveler. It is silent. Whether you are a wound in the world or the bandage that will close it... that is what the spirits must decide."
