# Spec: World & Narrative Design

## Goals
1. **Flesh out the "Magic-Tech" conflict:** Define the specific friction between the Iron Syndicate and the Adepts.
2. **Character Development:** Create detailed profiles for Overseer Vane, Elder Syla, and expand on Kaelen.
3. **Narrative Roadmap:** Outline the "Shattered Arrival" arc and the transition to the "Nexus" city.
4. **DLC/Expansion Hooks:** Identify "Unsolved Mysteries" that can be used for future content.

## Key Deliverables
- `characters.md`: Profiles, motivations, and seeded personalities for key NPCs.
- `world-bible-expansion.md`: Deep dives into "Spirit Burnout," "Static Spots," and "The Fall."
- `storylet-logic.md`: A mapping of how these narrative elements trigger specific gameplay effects.
