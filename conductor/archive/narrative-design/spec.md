# Specification: World Building & Storylets

## 1. Lore Shell
- **Setting**: Eldoria (The Borderlands).
- **Core Conflict**: Resonance (Sanctuary) vs. Distortion (Exploitation).
- **Magic Source**: Aetheria (Shattered Spirits).

## 2. Technical QBN Requirements
- Storylets must support `prerequisites` for Alignment, Purity, and Location.
- Choice effects must support `revealNames` and `revealKnowledge`.
- Content must support `{npc:id}` and `{flag:key}` interpolation.

## 3. High-Level Arcs
- **The Arrival**: The Null Meteor impact and Kaelen encounter.
- **The Outpost**: Introduction to the Syndicate and the Black Market.
- **The Sovereign**: Uncovering the Shattered Sovereign's influence.
